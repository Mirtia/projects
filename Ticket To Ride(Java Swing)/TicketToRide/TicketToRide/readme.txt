How the game works, what to expect etc...
------------------------------------------
Player's Turn:

TrainCards from the Railyard to the OntheTrack are moved automatically every Player's turn.

Then the player has 3 options:

i)Buy DestinationsCards card
ONLY 1!

ii)Take TrainCards from FaceUp or TrainDeck.
ONLY 2!

iii)Move Cards on Railyard
ONCE!

!WARNING!
---When you take a TrainCards card from either of choise (ii) you are obliged to take another one.---

-------------------------------------------

The objective is to obtain more points than your opponent.

Be careful , there may be traps while playing!
